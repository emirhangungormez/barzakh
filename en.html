Barzakh: Star Gardener

Barzakh is a puzzle game based on traveling through different times and places to find the hidden stars in the universe.

On your inner journey, you collect stars and grow a garden in your mind. Explore the metaphysical boundaries and discover the poetic spirit of a traveler stranded between worlds.

What is Barzakh: Star Gardener?

Barzakh: Star Gardener is an atmospheric puzzle game inspired by Sufi mysticism and the poetic spirit of Antoine de Saint-Exupéry's timeless masterpiece, The Little Prince. Players take on the role of Emir, who finds himself stranded in a mysterious desert after a plane crash. He embarks on a metaphysical journey to find hidden stars across different times and places, exploring both the outer world and his own inner universe. Each star becomes a seed planted in the garden of Emir's mind, drawing players deeper into the story.

What does Barzakh mean?

Barzakh is an Arabic-origin word that in Islamic mysticism refers to the invisible veil or transitional realm between worldly life and the afterlife. In Sufism, this concept represents the thin boundary between physical and spiritual realms. In the game, Barzakh symbolizes Emir's spiritual journey, his search across universes, and his confrontation with himself. This mystical concept adds depth to both the visual aesthetics and narrative structure of the game.

Who is developing the game?

Barzakh: Star Gardener is being developed solo by independent game developer Emirhan Güngörmez under Han Interactive Entertainment. Emirhan handles all aspects of development single-handedly, including game design, programming, visual design, and story writing. This approach ensures the game maintains a consistent vision and reaches players with a personal touch.

Which game engine is it developed with?

Barzakh is being developed using Unreal Engine 5. This cutting-edge game engine delivers cinematic-quality visuals through its Lumen global illumination system and Nanite virtualized geometry technology. UE5's powerful tools provide the ideal platform for creating Barzakh's surrealist and metaphysical atmosphere.

How long is the gameplay?

Barzakh: Star Gardener is designed to offer approximately 2-3 hours of intense and meaningful gameplay experience. This duration is optimized for players to fully experience the story, solve puzzles, and immerse themselves in the game's atmosphere. Quality comes before quantity; every moment is designed to be valuable and unforgettable.

When will it be released?
